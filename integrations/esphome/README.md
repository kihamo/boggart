# Wemos D1 mini ESP8266
## Flash
- Connect WEMOS to USB-cable
- Place a lead between D3 and GND
- Press the small reset button
- Remove lead to D3
- You *should* be able upload firmware

# NodeMCU ESP32
## Flash
- Power ON
- Press and keep BOOT button
- You *should* be able upload firmware
